What do we do?

PRK was created to raise awareness of ethics and
sustainability in the fashion industry and to promote the
purchase and use of quality fashion items in reponse to
fast fashion.

What is fast fashion?

Fast fashion is the mass production of clothing with focus
on profits, resulting in an unprecedented amount of clothing
related waste in landfills and water sources and unethical
work situations in factories.

How can we fight fast fashion?

Ethical fashion starts with caring for higher quality pieces,
advocating for factory worker's rights, and resisting the urge
to buy cheap, mass-produced clothing from profit-focused
companies.
